A selection of images from Barron’s fifty one-of-a-kind artist’s books is in progress.

Her first book, another song (1981), is in an edition of 51 copies. Similarly, this book also consists
of original works, not reproductions. It was co-authored with composer John Cage, whose
poem was written to Barron’s sequence of photographs.

In the meantime all book-works can be viewed by appointment via the Contact page.
